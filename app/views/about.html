In our continued commitment to ensuring the adequacy, security, diversity, and cost-effectiveness of the Commonwealth's energy supply within the context of creating a cleaner energy future, the Department of Energy Resources (DOER) is pleased to present the Energy Incentive and Rebate Portal. The Energy Incentive and Rebate Portal is a one-stop-shop that consolidates the energy incentives offered by a variety of utility plants and energy organizations in Massachusetts.

Our motivation to create this portal is to provide a comprehensive list of incentives and rebates available to the public. This portal provides the opportunity to search, learn, and apply for these cost-saving incentives and rebates. By entering your zip code, you will be guided to incentives and rebates in your area.
